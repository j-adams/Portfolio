About Me

My name is Jennifer Adams. I am currently 16 and attend Montgomery High School. My intellectual interests include
computer science (I love coding!), graphic design, math, and many sciences (especially physics!). I play and have played soccer
since I was 4 years old. I currently play for both the Jersey Knights and my high school team. I also love running and, aside from running as a hobby,
I run for my high school track team. This past year I joined my local fire company and am officially a junior volunteer firefighter. More of my hobbies include
painting, jewelry making (I love being able to see my work getting use!), water color, and most importantly, being able to make my friends laugh.
